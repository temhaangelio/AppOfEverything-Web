About

AppOfEverything is a comprehensive and user-friendly mobile application designed to simplify your daily life. We bring together all the tools you need in one place, from task management to habit tracking, budget control to shopping lists.

With its minimalist and elegant design, our app offers maximum efficiency without complexity, providing the easiest way to organize your life. Every feature has been carefully developed with user experience at the forefront.

Features

Task Management

Create tasks, set alarms, and complete them. View your past tasks in a separate section.

Habit Tracking

Track your daily habits. Choose which days to do them, set daily goals, and see your progress on the calendar.

Shopping List

Add your shopping items, specify quantities and units. Mark items you've purchased.

Budget Tracking

Record your income and expenses. View your financial status on a daily, weekly, monthly, or yearly basis.

Your Privacy is Our Priority

Your data is securely encrypted and stored in a secure cloud infrastructure. Only you can access your personal data. We don't collect or track any data - your privacy is our guarantee. We never share or sell your information to third parties.

Encrypted Security

Your data is protected with end-to-end encryption in the cloud

Zero Tracking

No usage data, analytics, or ad tracking whatsoever

Yours, Not Ours

Only you can access your data, never shared with anyone
