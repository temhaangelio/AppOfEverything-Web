Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 15, 2024

1. Introduction

AppOfEverything ("App", "We", "Our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Email Address: For account creation and login
  • Username: For profile management
  • Name and Surname: For personalization (optional)

2.2 Application Usage Data

  • Tasks: Tasks you create and completion status
  • Habits: Habits you track and progress data
  • Shopping Lists: Shopping lists you create
  • Budget Data: Your income and expense records
  • Notifications: Reminders and alarms you set

2.3 Technical Data

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version
  • App Usage: Which features are used and frequency
  • Error Reports: For app performance and error analysis

3. How We Collect Information

3.1 Direct Collection

  • Information you provide when creating an account
  • Content you create within the app
  • Your settings and preferences

3.2 Automatic Collection

  • Usage statistics through Firebase Analytics
  • Error reports through Crashlytics
  • Performance monitoring data

4. How We Use Your Information

4.1 Service Provision

  • Operating app features
  • Syncing your data
  • Providing personalized experience

4.2 Communication

  • Account security notifications
  • Important app updates
  • Providing customer support

4.3 Development

  • Improving app performance
  • Developing new features
  • Bug fixes

5. Data Sharing

5.1 Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google): Data storage and analytics
  • Apple App Store: App distribution and payments
  • Expo: Development and distribution platform

5.2 Data Sales

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for commercial purposes.

6. Data Security

6.1 Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Regular security updates
  • Access control and authentication

6.2 Data Retention

  • Your data is stored as long as your account is active
  • When account is deleted, data is completely removed within 30 days
  • Some data may be retained longer due to legal obligations

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our app does not use cookies, but we use the following technologies:

  • Firebase Analytics: Usage statistics
  • Crashlytics: Error reports
  • Local Storage: App settings and preferences

8. User Rights

8.1 Right to Access

  • You can request access to your personal information
  • You can obtain a copy of your data

8.2 Right to Rectification

  • You can correct incorrect or incomplete information
  • You can update your profile information

8.3 Right to Erasure

  • You can delete your account at any time
  • You can request deletion of your data

8.4 Right to Restriction

  • You can restrict data processing activities
  • You can stop processing of certain data

9. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete that information immediately.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data is stored on Firebase servers, which may be located in different countries. Data transfers are made with appropriate security measures.
